Help & FAQ - PixelBoost Online

How do I use PixelBoost Online?

1. Upload: Click "Choose Image" to select an image from your device.
2. Configure: Use the tabs to:

  • Resize & Compress: Adjust dimensions, aspect ratio, resize mode (Stretch, Fit, Expand), and compression quality. Click "Update Preview".
  • Watermark Remover: Drag on the main image preview (top) to select the watermark. Then click "Apply Basic Removal". This works on the current previewed image.
3. Download: Once you're happy with the preview in Section 3, click "Download Image".

What are the resize modes?

Stretch: Forces image to new dimensions, may distort.
Fit (Letterbox): Fits image within new dimensions, maintains aspect ratio, adds padding if needed (you can choose padding color).
Expand (Crop): Fills new dimensions, maintains aspect ratio, crops parts of image if needed.

How effective is the Watermark Remover?

It's a basic tool. It works best on watermarks over simple, relatively uniform backgrounds. It attempts to fill the selected area with the average color of its surrounding border. Complex watermarks or those on intricate backgrounds may not be removed cleanly. We recommend trying it on a copy of your image first!

What image formats are supported?

You can upload PNG, JPEG, WebP, and GIF files. Output is generally PNG, or JPEG/WebP if compression is applied to those types. Animated GIFs will be processed as static images (first frame).

Is it free? How do you make money?

Yes, PixelBoost Online is completely free to use! We aim to support the site through non-intrusive advertisements and voluntary donations (like "Buy Me a Coffee").

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. All image processing happens directly in your web browser. Your images never leave your computer, ensuring your privacy.
